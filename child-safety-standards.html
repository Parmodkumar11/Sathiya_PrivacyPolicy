Parinay Sanskar Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Parinay Sanskar is dedicated to providing a safe and secure environment for all users, especially minors. We take a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Prohibited Content

Our app strictly prohibits any content involving child exploitation, abuse, or illegal activities. Violations will result in immediate account suspension and reporting to the relevant authorities.

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, please report it via our in-app reporting feature or email us directly at safety@Parinay Sanskar.com.

Compliance and Reporting

We comply with all regional and national child safety laws and cooperate with law enforcement to report any incidents of CSAE. Our team is dedicated to ensuring compliance and supporting safety initiatives.

Prevention Measures

Parinay Sanskar implements advanced moderation tools, user verification systems, and manual content reviews to protect users from harmful content. We are continually improving our safeguards to maintain the highest standards of safety.
